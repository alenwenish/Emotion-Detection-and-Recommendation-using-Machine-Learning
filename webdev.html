What is Text Emotion Detector ?????

-- It detects the emotion of the entered text and predict whether the given sentence is joy😃, sadness😓, anger😠, fear😨,
love😘 or surprise😮.
-- Based on the predicted emotions , the recommendations will suggested for the user in the form of video, song, quotes .etc.



How it works ?????

Step 1 : Click on the link given below to enter into the emotion detector page.
Step 2 : Enter the text or sentence in the dialog box provided in the page.
Step 3 : Click on the submit button and wait for few seconds to analyze your text.
Step 4 : Emotion related to your text is displayed and recommendations are provided in the form of video, songs and quotes.
Step 5 : If you wish to go through the link , Click on it and reveal your emotions.
